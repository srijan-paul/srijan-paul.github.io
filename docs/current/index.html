This month, I was busy setting base in Bangalore. Moving is a lot of work, and I'm taking my time getting used to the city - loads of people, incessant honking on roads, metros operating at twice their capacity, and a language foreign to me.

I'm sure there's a lot more that will be to my liking, and I'm just taking my time getting used to it.
